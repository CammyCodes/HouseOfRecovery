Our Philosophy

Skincare is health.·Recovery is discipline.

House of Recovery isn't a spa. We don't dim the lights, pour prosecco, or call our work a treat. We measure what changes — fluid moved, contractions delivered, congestion cleared, resilience built.

Every session is prescribed, not improvised. Whether you arrive an athlete in mid-block, a mother in the months after birth, or simply tired of carrying the small accumulations of modern life, you leave with a protocol. We build resilience. You build life.
